I like it because its so delicious and has a nice scent.

The East African Pilau

East African Pilau Rice is quick, easy flavored packed vegan rice with tons of spices.

Great for meat lovers too!

The ingridients needed include;
  • 2-3 tablespoons cooking oil/butter/Ghee
  • 1 teaspoons cumin spice
  • ¼ teaspoon curry spice
  • ½ teaspoon star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon pepper flake optional
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  • 1 small jalapenos pepper deseeded and chopped
  • 1/3 -1/2 cup cashew optional
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1-2 tomatoes chopped
  • 1 small red bell pepper chopped
  • 4 cups Liquid Broth or water, and or coconut milk
  • 1½ teaspoon salt or more adjust to taste
Steps to prepare the dish.
  1. Heat a saucepan with oil/ butter. Sauté the cashews for about 2 -3 minutes.😁
  2. Then add all the spices – cumin, cinnamon stick, bay leaf, curry, cardamom and paprika, stir for about 1 minute.
  3. Throw in the garlic, ginger, jalapeños pepper and onions.🤭
  4. Continue cooking for another minute.
  5. Add tomatoes,bell pepper and continue cooking for about 2-3 minutes.😍
  6. Stir in rice , cook for about 2 minutes, then add 4 cups of broth /coconut milk, salt and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat, cover and simmer until rice is completely cooked- about 18- 20 minutes.😋
  8. As the rice cooks you may add more stock if needed.
  9. Fluffy, remove cinnamon stick, bay leaf and serve. 🥘
